Introduction

TEYSEER CEP DIVISION

TEYSEER Trading & Contracting Company (TTCC) began to establish in Qatar in 1968. TEYSEER Chemicals, Engineering & Projects (CEP) is a subsidiary of TTCC. CEP Division is basically a leading supplier and services provider to oil, gas, petrochemical, power generation, desalination and infrastructure sectors in Qatar.

Within the global companies represented by TEYSEER CEP Division, we can serve the Qatari market in different ways. The companies are represented in the CEP portfolio are represented in Qatar, through Commercial Agency Agreements, are divided into three main categories (fields) as follows: Mechanical, Chemical & Building Materials.
